EasyClimb.tech Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy (the “Policy”) describes how EasyClimb.tech (“EasyClimb.tech,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and discloses the personal information you provide when using our Service. The term “Service” includes the website www.easyclimb.tech (the “Website”) along with its subdomains and any related content, software, applications, materials, and/or services made available by EasyClimb.tech through the Website.

Use of Google User Data

If you choose to link your Google Calendar with our Service, we will request access to your calendar events. We only collect the start and end times of your events to facilitate interview scheduling. We do not access event names, attendee lists, locations, or any sensitive content. We will also request to perform writes to your calendar in the form of your scheduled session events.

How We Use Google User Data

Google user data accessed via the Calendar API is used exclusively to help you schedule and manage mock interviews within the EasyClimb.tech platform. We do not use this data for marketing or advertising purposes.

Sharing and Disclosure of Google User Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your Google Calendar data with any third parties. The data is used solely for scheduling within our application. We may share anonymized, aggregated usage analytics, but these never contain personally identifiable or Google account information.

Data Protection

We take reasonable and appropriate security measures to protect your Google user data, including encryption in transit and at rest, access control, and secure authentication mechanisms. Only authorized personnel may access this data.

Data Retention and Deletion

Calendar data retrieved from Google is stored only as long as necessary to provide the scheduling functionality. Users can revoke access at any time through their Google account. If you disconnect your Google Calendar, all related data is deleted from our system within 30 days.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting support@easyclimb.tech.

Compliance with Google API Services User Data Policy

EasyClimb.tech’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
